About Us

LeadShare is an international platform created for student leaders, by student leaders. Our mission is to help student councils around the world share their most creative ideas, collaborate on impactful events, and access resources that elevate school communities. Whether you're planning a rally or brainstorming a fundraiser, LeadShare is your space to learn and lead globally.
